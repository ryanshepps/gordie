## The problem with fantasy hockey tools

Most fantasy hockey tools follow the same pattern: sign in to a dashboard, navigate tabs, parse tables, compare projections. It works, but it demands your time and attention at the tool's convenience, not yours.

Between work, life, and the 10 other things on your plate, opening yet another app to figure out if you should start your backup goalie tonight isn't realistic for most people.

## Why email works better

Email meets you where you already are. You check it dozens of times a day. It's on every device. It doesn't require a login, a download, or a new habit.

With Gordie, getting fantasy hockey advice is as simple as sending a message:

- **"Should I start Demko or Swayman tonight?"** — Gordie checks the matchups, recent performance, and schedule, then replies with a recommendation.
- **"Who should I pick up from waivers?"** — Gordie scans available players, evaluates fit with your roster, and ranks your options.
- **"Is my team in trouble for playoffs?"** — Gordie looks at your standings, schedule, and roster health to give you an honest assessment.

No tabs. No navigation. Just a question and an answer.

## Conversations have context

Unlike a dashboard where every visit starts from scratch, email threads preserve context. Gordie remembers what you asked last week. If you follow up on a trade discussion from three days ago, Gordie picks up right where you left off.

This conversational context makes the advice better over time. Gordie learns your roster, your league's dynamics, and your decision-making style.

## Built for busy people

We built Gordie for the fantasy hockey manager who loves their league but doesn't have hours to spend on research. The kind of person who wants to make smart moves but needs the insights delivered, not discovered.
