## What is Gordie?

Gordie is an AI-powered fantasy hockey assistant that works entirely through email. You email Gordie a question about your fantasy team, and Gordie replies with data-driven advice — like having a fantasy hockey analyst on speed dial.

## Step 1: Sign up

Head to [gordie.lastingsoftware.ca](/) and enter your email address. Within a minute, you'll get a welcome email from Gordie.

## Step 2: Connect your Yahoo Fantasy account

Gordie's welcome email includes a link to securely connect your Yahoo Fantasy account. This gives Gordie read-only access to your teams, so it can see your roster, league settings, and standings.

**Important:** Gordie only reads your data. It will never make roster changes, accept trades, or drop players on your behalf.

## Step 3: Ask your first question

Once connected, just reply to Gordie's email with any fantasy hockey question. Here are some good ones to start with:

- **"Give me a rundown of my team"** — Gordie will analyze your roster strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement.
- **"Who should I start tonight?"** — Get lineup recommendations based on today's matchups.
- **"Any good waiver pickups for me?"** — Gordie scans available players and suggests fits for your roster.

## What to expect

Gordie typically responds within a couple of minutes. The response will include relevant stats, reasoning, and clear recommendations. If you want more detail, just ask a follow-up question in the same thread.

## Tips for getting the best advice

- **Be specific when you can.** "Should I start Hellebuyck or Saros tonight?" gets a more targeted answer than "Who should I start?"
- **Mention trade offers in detail.** "I was offered Pasta for my Rantanen + a 3rd round pick" gives Gordie everything it needs.
- **Ask follow-ups.** Gordie remembers context within a conversation, so feel free to drill deeper.
